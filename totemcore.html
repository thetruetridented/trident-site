simple & lightweight

This is a simple auto totem that works differently from other auto totems: it opens your inventory, moves your cursor toward a totem, then equips that totem to offhand with configurable randomness, delay, and speed.

WANT MORE?

If installed serverside, this mod acts as its own anti-cheat and searches for totemcore:totem-rep to remove you from the server. The packet is not present if you compile from source or get it from Patreon.

You also get access to in dev features and exclusive features from the Patreon.
