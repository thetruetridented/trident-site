simple & lightweight

This is a clientside utility mod for mace combat that helps you use the mace and related items to their full potential without rattling your brain to the core. The free version is singleplayer-focused unless you get the full Patreon version.

WANT MORE?

If installed serverside, this mod acts as its own anti-cheat and searches for macecore:macecore-rep to remove you from the server. The packet is not present if you compile from source or get it from Patreon.

The 1.21.4 and 1.21.1 versions are Patreon-exclusive, along with in dev and exclusive features.

features

Pearl Catching

Works while aiming diagonally and is designed around fast midair mace setups.

Shield Slamming

Disable a shield into a mace hit while midair for cleaner mace pressure.

Sword & Axe Swapping

Dynamically chooses which mace to use for attribute swapping based on position and velocity.

Lock-on Assist

A soft aim assist that can be fully configured.

Shield Draining

Uses mace timing to destroy shield durability when an opponent is blocking.

Tick Variance

Most features include configurable tick variance to minimize detection patterns.
