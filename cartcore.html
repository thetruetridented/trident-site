simple & lightweight

This is a clientside utility mod for cart combat that helps you use TNT minecarts and bows to their full potential without rattling your brain to the core. It is intended for small scale singleplayer worlds and small scale servers.

WANT MORE?

If installed serverside, this mod acts as its own anti-cheat and searches for cartcore:cartcore-rep to remove you from the server. The packet is not present if you compile from source or get it from Patreon.

The 1.21.4 and 1.21.1 versions will be Patreon-exclusive, along with in dev and exclusive features.

features

Insta-carting

Look somewhat downward and right click. The mod releases your bow at the right time for a proper insta-cart.

Cart Overloading

Places multiple carts on the same block for larger detonations and occasional launched carts.

Crossbow Carting

Places rail, TNT minecart, and flint and steel with a viewpoint flick before the original shot registers.

Intuitive Sound Effects

Custom vanilla-based sound effects trigger when cart tech is performed.

Tick Variance

Most features include configurable tick variance to minimize detection patterns.
