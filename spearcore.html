This is a clientside mod for the newest snapshots that uses attribute swapping tech to perform a dash or roll on your chosen keybind. As long as you have a spear enchanted with Lunge in your hotbar, you can dash forward, backward, sideways, and diagonally.

WANT MORE?

The premium version includes range extension-swapping: if a spear without Lunge is in your inventory and you attack a mob just outside normal reach, a spear swap is attempted to bridge the gap.

You also get access to in dev features and exclusive features from the Patreon.
